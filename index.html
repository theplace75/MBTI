MBTI

Byungkwan Chae (first author), Youngjae Choi*, Heewon Kim†
Soongsil University, Seoul, Republic of Korea
WACV 2026

Abstract

MBTI improves fine-grained diffusion personalization by promoting cross-class separation in the text-embedding space.

Method

SES selects confusing neighbors; DMM maximizes diffusion-based distance; the objective balances separation and fidelity.

Qualitative Results

MBTI preserves fine-grained details where baselines/SoTA methods often blur or distort them.

Quantitative Results

MBTI boosts few-shot Macro-ACC and improves FID/CLIP as a plug-and-play add-on to LoRA and DreamBooth+LoRA.
